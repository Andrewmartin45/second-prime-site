“I haven’t felt this good in almost 20 years.”

Where he started

Dustin had spent nine years building his construction company, raising six kids, and volunteering at his church and with local youth sports. He poured everything into the business and his family, and the one person he never took care of was himself.

Everything in life was going well, except how he felt. Energy was gone by mid-afternoon. He was 290 pounds, in a size 40 waist and XXL shirts, with self-esteem at a low and a gut that left him bloated and miserable after meals. It had felt off for more than a year before he did something about it.

What he’d already tried

A personal trainer. TRT. Tracking his food. Supplements. “Nothing would stick or work.” The pieces were never coordinated, and nobody was reading them together.

What the data revealed

“It would probably be easier to say what wasn’t tested.” Blood, urine, stool, and saliva. Body scans, fitness assessments, even a VO₂ max. Read together, the picture was clear in a way no single test had ever shown him.

“I didn’t realize how unhealthy I was, or how much that was driving how I was feeling.” High liver enzymes. Systemic inflammation. Gut health all over the board. Low testosterone. Triglycerides that were nearly off the chart.

The plan

Nutrition. A disciplined process: tracking exactly what he ate and treating his daily targets like a game to win, with Andrew’s planning sheets to guide each week.

Training. They started slow, just moving every day and a few basic gym sessions a week, then built from there as his body responded.

Supplements & therapies. A low dose of a RETA peptide early on, plus vitamin D, a multivitamin, omega-3s, curcumin, berberine, NAC, and magnesium at night, all adjusted as his labs changed.

Coaching. Regular calls and text access whenever questions came up. “Encouraging, yet motivating, in how he would challenge me.” Every decision driven by the data, week to week and quarter to quarter.
