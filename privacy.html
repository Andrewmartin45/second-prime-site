Who we are

Second Prime ("we," "us," "our") provides done-for-you health optimization for founders and executives. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we keep it secure. Questions: privacy@secondprime.com.

What we collect

  • Contact details you give us: name, email, phone, and anything you share when you book a consult or request a guide.
  • Health information you provide or authorize us to collect as part of your program: lab results, biomarkers, intake forms, symptom and lifestyle data, and notes from your consultations.
  • Usage data from this website: pages viewed and general analytics, collected through cookies and similar tools.

How we use it

  • To build, run, and adjust your personalized protocol.
  • To coordinate testing, scheduling, and shipments on your behalf.
  • To communicate with you about your program and, if you opt in, to send educational content. You can unsubscribe at any time.

How it's stored and who has access

Your data is stored on secured, access-controlled systems. Access is limited to your Second Prime team and the licensed medical partners involved in your care. We do not sell your personal or health information.

Health-data handling

Where applicable, protected health information is handled in line with relevant health-privacy regulations (for example, HIPAA in the United States), including any agreements with our medical partners. [Confirm exact obligations with counsel.]

Cookies and analytics

We use cookies and analytics to understand how the site is used and to improve it. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Your choices

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and clinical record-keeping requirements. Email privacy@secondprime.com.
