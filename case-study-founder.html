“Honestly, I came in skeptical. I’d done bloodwork a dozen times. Andrew found things in week one that nobody had ever flagged.”

Where he started

A 44-year-old tech founder running on caffeine and adrenaline. Annual physicals always came back “normal.” But the afternoon crashes were getting worse, the midsection wouldn’t budge no matter the diet, and the focus that built his company was slipping.

What we found

His standard panel looked fine. Ours didn’t. ApoB was driving silent cardiovascular risk a decade ahead of any symptom, and hs-CRP showed systemic inflammation his physical never measured. Two of the four pillars, already in motion at 44.

The plan

A targeted lipid protocol, an anti-inflammatory nutrition and supplement stack, and a training block built around his travel schedule. Re-tested at 12 weeks, adjusted, re-tested again.
